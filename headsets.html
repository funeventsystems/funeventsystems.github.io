Ultra-Low Latency Communication System

For Events, Sports, and More

Transform Your Communication

Introducing our revolutionary Ultra-Low Latency Communication System — built with open-source technology and designed for live events, corporate gatherings, and athletic sports like football and hockey. Whether you’re a coach, referee, event organizer, or broadcaster, our system ensures seamless, real-time communication, no matter where you are.

Who Can Use This System?

  • Live Events: Keep everyone connected with crystal-clear audio from speakers to the crew, staff, and attendees.
  • Corporate Events: Ensure smooth communication for your event team, whether you’re running a seminar or conference.
  • Athletic Sports: Coaches and refs can communicate instantly, while fans stay involved with calls broadcasted live.

Key Features

  • Ultra-Low Latency: Tested with up to 10 users, with a theoretical limit of 100 users. High-quality communication even in larger groups.
  • Works Anywhere: No internet required for local use. Internet allows global communication (testing ongoing).
  • Easy Setup: BYOD — simply pair mobile phones, tablets, or desktops to Bluetooth headsets.
  • Affordable & Open Source: Built on open-source Mumble technology, providing reliability without the expensive price tag.

How It Works

The system operates through a Mumble-based server, connected to Bluetooth headsets. Participants use their own mobile devices, tablets, or desktops, paired via Bluetooth. Whether for events or sports, communication is instant and clear, even without the internet. Global connectivity is possible when connected via the internet.
