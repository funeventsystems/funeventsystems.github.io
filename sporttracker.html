Live Sports Analytics Technology

Real-Time Player Tracking & Performance Insights

Key Features

Our Live Sports Analytics Technology brings coaches and players real-time, actionable insights that help optimize performance and training. It’s perfect for indoor sports like hockey**, where player movement, speed, and position are critical.

  • Wearable Bluetooth Low-Energy (BTLE) Bands: Each player wears a customized wristband that sends real-time data (speed, distance, explosiveness) to the receivers around the venue. The data is then displayed on an iPad, allowing coaches to track all players’ movements.
  • Real-Time Insights: Coaches and players can view live performance metrics on a dashboard or mobile app, helping to make immediate decisions during training and games.
  • Video Integration: Player data can be tagged to video footage for personalized highlight reels and detailed game analysis. Players and coaches can review performance after the game, as well as live during the game.
  • Referee Call Integration (Coming Soon!): We plan to integrate referee calls into the data, so you can analyze player positions and movement at the time of a call for deeper tactical analysis.
  • Customizable for Teams: The system can be customized to track the specific player metrics that matter most to your team and coaching staff.
  • Rentable Kit: Rent the system for a single game or an entire season. The rental kit includes everything you need to start tracking player performance and gathering data.

The wristband can also be customized to match your team, and branding for the team. We also have a few different styles, so contact us to see what would work best for you. We even have one that fits into the jersey itself!
