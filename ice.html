MindMateListens Ice Bucket Challenge

Help us donate 100 licenses to students in need, just by taking part!

How It Works

  • Record your Ice Bucket Challenge video and tag us on Instagram (@mastermindsdrama) or email it to funlightingsystems@gmail.com.
  • For every video you submit, we’ll donate two licenses to a school or charity of your choice.
  • If you’re participating as a team, we’ll donate two licenses for every person—10 people = 20 licenses donated!
Get Involved

Special Prize: Bishop Carroll Spring Show Tickets

Every participant gets a chance to win one of five tickets to the Bishop Carroll Spring Show! Select your night and enjoy an evening of theater!

Our Goal: 100 Licenses

We aim to donate 100 licenses to schools and charities, helping students access a safe, anonymous space to talk about their mental health. Let’s make a real difference together!

About the Speak Your Mind Challenge

This campaign runs alongside the **Speak Your Mind Challenge**, which is currently raising awareness for mental health. However, we want to clarify that MindMateListens is not affiliated with the **Speak Your Mind Challenge**. We are simply supporting the larger conversation around mental health by offering a way to make a direct impact through our donation of licenses.

By participating in the Ice Bucket Challenge for Mental Health, you’re helping us create more safe spaces for students to talk through their mental health concerns, whether anonymously or directly. Thank you for your support!
